# Weekly Automation Brief: Competitive Intelligence Tracker

## Objective
Run a weekly public-data competitive intelligence tracker for volunteering, conservation, school camps, scuba diving, corporate CSR, adventure and eco-tourism experiences in Malaysia and Southeast Asia.

The automation should behave like a senior online marketing analyst: skeptical, evidence-driven, practical and commercially focused. It must judge how well each organisation attracts attention, ranks in search, builds trust, converts visitors, communicates value, uses social media, creates content, runs campaigns and improves over time.

Use public information only: websites, search results, visible social posts, public ad libraries, public videos, blogs, reviews, directories, backlinks, landing pages and business listings. Do not use private logins, impersonation, fake enquiries, restricted scraping, hacking or private data.

## Core Questions
- Who is strongest online?
- Who is improving fastest?
- Who is actively marketing?
- Who is running visible ads?
- Who is posting on social media and LinkedIn?
- Who is publishing blogs or videos?
- Who is winning on SEO and AEO?
- Who has the best website, clearest offer, strongest pricing, best trust signals and strongest conversion flow?
- What can Fuze Ecoteer learn from them?
- Where is Fuze Ecoteer better?
- Where is Fuze Ecoteer weaker?
- What should Fuze Ecoteer do next to beat them?

## Scoring
Score each organisation out of 100:
- Website and conversion: 15
- Social media and community: 15
- SEO and AEO performance: 20
- YouTube and video presence: 10
- Offer, cost and value positioning: 10
- Location, logistics and safety: 8
- Trust, credibility and impact: 7
- Active marketing and campaign momentum: 10
- Strategic learning and competitive threat: 5

Also calculate Active Marketing Score out of 50 and Momentum Score out of 20.

Trend labels: Improving, Stable, Declining, Dormant, Emerging threat, Benchmark leader, Opportunity to beat, Needs review.

## SEO, AEO, Keyword And Backlink Requirements
Track keyword phrases, intent, titles, meta descriptions, headings, FAQ presence, schema signals, local keywords, long-tail questions, answer quality, keyword density diagnostic notes, content gaps, backlinks, referring domains, best links, relevant school/university/NGO/government/media links and backlink opportunities for Fuze Ecoteer.

AEO checks should ask whether pages clearly answer: what the project is, where it is, who it is for, cost, inclusions, duration, activities, requirements, diving certification, school suitability, corporate suitability, accommodation, meals, transport, safety, impact, best time to join and how to enquire.

## Social And Ads Requirements
Check Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, LinkedIn, YouTube, Threads, X, Pinterest and Google Business Profile where relevant. Track visible post frequency, reels/videos, themes, use of volunteers/wildlife/schools/corporates/staff/testimonials/education/offers, engagement quality, CTA and strongest/weakest post.

Check public ad signals such as Meta Ad Library, Google Ads Transparency Center, TikTok Creative Center, visible sponsored posts and LinkedIn sponsored content where publicly visible.

## Report Requirements
Every weekly report should include Executive Summary, New Discoveries, Category Leaderboards, Competitive Spy Summary, Mowgli Venture Watch, SEO and AEO Summary, Social and Ads Summary, What We Can Learn, How We Are Better and Recommended Actions.

Every recommendation must be practical and evidence-led. The automation should not only say what changed; it should say why it matters and what Fuze Ecoteer should do next.
