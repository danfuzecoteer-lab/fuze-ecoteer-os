# Weekly Automation Brief: Deep Research for Grants, CSR Funds, Sponsorships and Conservation Funding

## Objective
Create a weekly automation that searches for, researches, scores, tracks and recommends grant, CSR, sponsorship and funding opportunities suitable for Fuze Ecoteer, Perhentian Turtle Project, Upcycled by FE and related conservation, community, education, marine and circular economy projects.

The automation must identify opportunities from corporate CSR funds, corporate foundations, corporate ESG programmes, Malaysian and Singapore government grants, government-linked organisations, embassies and high commissions, international conservation funds, marine conservation grants, circular economy and plastic waste grants, community conservation grants, climate and biodiversity funds, youth and education funds, university-linked research or fieldwork grants, tourism and social enterprise funds, private family foundations, ASEAN funding programmes, UN-linked funds and development agencies.

The automation should do deep research. Do not dump weak links into a list. Collect practical information such as application dates, deadlines, opening periods, eligibility, application links, visible form questions, required documents, grant titles, funding amount, geographic focus, project themes, chance of success, recommended project angle, suggested internal owner, next action and whether the opportunity is worth pursuing.

## Scoring System
Score each opportunity out of 100.

### Project Fit: 25 points
- Thematic fit: 8
- Geographic fit: 5
- Organisation fit: 5
- Project maturity fit: 4
- Impact fit: 3

### Chance of Success: 20 points
- Eligibility clarity: 5
- Past funding pattern: 4
- Relationship potential: 3
- Competition level: 3
- Evidence strength: 3
- Timing suitability: 2

### Funding Value: 15 points
- Grant size: 5
- Cost coverage: 4
- Flexibility: 3
- Repeat or scale potential: 3

### Application Effort: 15 points
Lower effort scores higher.

- Application complexity: 4
- Document requirements: 3
- Budget complexity: 3
- Partnership requirement: 3
- Reporting burden: 2

### Strategic Value: 15 points
- Credibility value: 4
- Partnership value: 3
- Visibility value: 3
- Project development value: 3
- Fundraising leverage: 2

### Urgency: 10 points
- Deadline proximity: 4
- Preparation readiness: 3
- Time-sensitive opportunity: 3

## Rating Bands
- 90 to 100: Apply immediately
- 75 to 89: High priority
- 60 to 74: Consider applying
- 40 to 59: Monitor or nurture
- Below 40: Low priority

## Chance Rating
Use High chance, Medium-high chance, Medium chance, Low-medium chance, Low chance or Unknown. Explain the reason based on eligibility, theme fit, past similar grants, evidence strength, application difficulty, relationship with the funder, competition level, deadline readiness, funding size and whether Fuze Ecoteer already has a suitable project.

## Effort Rating
Use Low effort, Medium effort, High effort, Very high effort or Not enough information. Base this on form length, required documents, budget detail, partner letters, co-funding, registration requirements, reporting burden, technical proposal requirements, new project design, financial statements, audited accounts and government support.
