# Weekly Automation Brief: Deep Client Research Agent

## Purpose
Run a weekly public-data research automation for Fuze Ecoteer CRM prospecting.

The automation must produce qualified lead intelligence for potential clients and referral partners. It must not write full outreach emails. A separate email-writing agent will use this research later.

The output should give the email-writing agent enough evidence, context, contact routes, LinkedIn signals, social signals and programme-fit analysis to write specific, useful outreach instead of generic sales sludge.

Weekly target:

- 20 school leads
- 20 tadika / preschool leads
- 20 university leads
- 20 corporate leads
- 20 network / referral partner leads

Minimum total: 100 researched leads per week.

Quality matters more than filling rows. Weak leads must be marked as lower priority or needing more research. Do not inflate weak evidence.

## Research Sources
Use only public, ethical and verifiable information:

- Organisation websites, contact pages, programme pages, news pages and blogs
- Annual reports, CSR reports, ESG reports and public PDFs
- School newsletters and university department pages
- Public staff directories
- LinkedIn company pages and public personal profiles
- Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, YouTube and Google Business Profile
- Public event pages, press releases and media articles
- Association directories, chamber listings, education directories and conference speaker lists
- Public reports, directories and public social posts

Check LinkedIn and social media carefully. Websites are often stale; recent posts can show what the organisation actually cares about now.

## Hard Rules
The automation must not:

- Write final outreach emails
- Message anyone or send connection requests
- Use private logins, restricted content or fake enquiries
- Scrape restricted content
- Use impersonation
- Collect private personal data
- Guess emails where no public pattern is available
- Invent contact names, interests, evidence or intent
- Claim a lead is interested without evidence
- Overstate weak evidence

Use public contact routes, department emails, enquiry forms, LinkedIn/company pages or source search phrases when direct contacts are not public.

## Deep Research Dossier Per Lead
Each lead must include these research points, compressed into the CRM row fields where needed:

- Organisation name
- Lead group
- Country and city / state
- Website
- LinkedIn page
- Instagram / Facebook / TikTok / YouTube links, where relevant
- Best public contact route
- Relevant person or department, where public
- Why this lead fits
- Evidence of fit
- Relevant recent activity
- Social media activity summary
- LinkedIn activity summary
- Likely decision-maker
- Suggested programme match
- Likely buyer motivation
- Possible pain point
- Estimated value potential
- Lead score
- Priority band
- Best timing to contact
- Key personalisation notes
- Source URLs
- Research confidence level

## Lead Scoring
Score each lead out of 100:

- Programme fit: 25
- Evidence of intent: 20
- Contactability: 15
- Value potential: 15
- Recent activity: 10
- Personalisation quality: 10
- Strategic value: 5

Priority bands:

- 90 to 100: Priority A
- 75 to 89: Priority B
- 60 to 74: Priority C
- 40 to 59: Nurture
- Below 40: Low priority

## CRM Row Output Requirements
The database row may have fewer columns than the full research dossier. When fields are limited, pack the extra intelligence into `research_notes`, `likely_need`, `recommended_offer`, `personalization_angle`, `next_action` and `source`.

Use these exact lead segment labels:

- School
- Tadika / Preschool
- University
- Corporate HR / CSR
- Network / Referral Partner

Use these exact JSON keys for each generated CRM row:

- lead_segment
- organisation_name
- country
- city
- website
- contact_department
- contact_name
- email
- linkedin_url
- research_notes
- likely_need
- recommended_offer
- personalization_angle
- priority
- next_action
- source
- confidence

Rules for the row:

- `priority` should include the band and score, for example `Priority A - 92/100`.
- `research_notes` must include evidence of fit, recent activity, LinkedIn/social summary, likely decision-maker, buyer motivation, pain point, timing, caution/uncertainty and source URLs.
- `personalization_angle` must be a hook for the future email-writing agent, not a finished email sentence.
- `source` must include source URLs or, where a source URL is not available, a transparent source/search phrase.
- `confidence` must be 0 to 1.
- `email` must be null unless a public and confident email is available.

## Notes For Email-Writing Agent
For every lead, include a clearly labelled notes section inside `research_notes`.

Include:

- Strongest personalisation hook
- Most relevant programme angle
- Likely decision-maker
- Best evidence to mention
- Tone to use
- Likely objection
- Best CTA type
- Any caution or uncertainty

No full email needed. Provide ammunition for the email agent.

Every week, generate 100 deeply researched, evidence-backed leads:

- 20 schools

- 20 networks / referral partners

The Network / Referral Partner group must include travel websites, travel agents, volunteer travel platforms, gap-year companies, responsible tourism outlets, travel media and referral/collaboration partners that can promote PTP, PMRS and PEEP.

Check websites, LinkedIn, social media, public reports, directories, news and public programme pages.

Produce ranked, scored, evidence-backed lead dossiers that another AI agent can use to write personalised outreach emails. Do not write the final emails.
